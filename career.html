CAREER JOURNEY

STEM Facilitator (2024–Present)

I began my professional journey in December 2024, taking on the role of a STEM Facilitator at Ispark Learning Solution Pvt. In this role, I work closely with students at Equitas Gurukul School, teaching robotics, IoT, electronics, coding, and hands-on project-based STEM concepts. This opportunity helped me understand how students think and how to simplify learning in a practical way.

As a facilitator, I plan weekly lessons, create project modules, demonstrate sensors and microcontrollers, and help students build real working models. This role strengthened my ability to communicate clearly, explain complex topics in simple ways, and support students during practical sessions.

Every week I handle multiple projects, guide students during experiments, troubleshoot electronics issues, and help them understand how components interact. These responsibilities improved my leadership, patience, and technical confidence.

This journey also helped me grow technically—I explored robotics kits, Arduino projects, IoT modules, and coding platforms used in school-level STEM curriculum. Working with students taught me valuable lessons about creativity, problem-solving, and how differently each student approaches learning.

Looking ahead, I aim to expand my knowledge in advanced IoT, automation, and AI-based educational tools. My future goal is to take on roles like STEM Program Coordinator or Senior Trainer, where I can design large-scale STEM programs and inspire more students.

Overall, my career from 2024 to now has been shaped by continuous learning, hands-on experience, and the desire to make technology simple, understandable, and enjoyable for students. I look forward to growing further and contributing more to STEM education in the future.
